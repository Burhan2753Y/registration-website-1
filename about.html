Welcome to Technovation — Burhani College's premier tech fest celebrating innovation, creativity, and technological excellence!


About Burhani College

Burhani College of Commerce & Arts, situated in the heart of Mazgaon, Mumbai, is a distinguished institution committed to providing quality education in commerce and arts. Established with a vision to foster academic excellence and personal growth, the college has been a nurturing ground for young talent.

With a focus on holistic development, Burhani College combines a strong academic curriculum with opportunities for extracurricular engagement, preparing students to excel in their chosen fields. The institution takes pride in creating a vibrant, inclusive environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and community involvement.

About Technovation

Set against the vibrant backdrop of Burhani College, renowned for nurturing talent and passion, this event promises to ignite the minds of tech enthusiasts and inspire them to push boundaries like never before.

Join us at Technovation, where coders, gamers, and problem-solvers converge to showcase their skills across five exhilarating events. Compete for glory and take home trophies and medals as a testament to your achievements. From thrilling hackathons to action-packed gaming tournaments, there's something for everyone to experience, challenge, and celebrate!
