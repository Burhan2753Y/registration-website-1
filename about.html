About the Event


Welcome to technovation, Burhani College's premier tech fest designed to showcase innovation, creativity, and technological prowess. Set in the vibrant atmosphere of Burhani College, a prestigious institution known for nurturing talent in various fields, this event promises to challenge and inspire students to push the boundaries of technology.

Taking place on technovation,, the event brings together coders, gamers, and problem-solvers to compete for a total prize pool of ₹69420. Participants will have the opportunity to prove their skills across five exciting events, each offering its own unique challenge. Whether you're aiming for the ultimate hackathon glory or looking to win in a fast-paced gaming tournament, this event has something for everyone!

HackVenture: The Ultimate Hackathon

HackVenture is the perfect platform for teams of developers, designers, and innovators to come together and build cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems. Over a span of 24 hours, you will work collaboratively to create a project from scratch, showcasing your technical creativity and teamwork skills. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned hacker, Hackventure welcomes all!

Code In The Dark: Blind Coding Challenge

This intense coding battle will put your coding skills to the ultimate test. With no access to previews, you will code a web page based solely on a given design. It's all about precision and speed, but there's a catch: you won’t see your progress until time’s up! This challenge is not for the faint-hearted but perfect for those who thrive under pressure.

BGMI Showdown: Battlegrounds Mobile India Tournament

For all gaming enthusiasts, our BGMI Showdown promises adrenaline-pumping action. Compete in squads and battle it out to be the last team standing. With high-stakes matches and thrilling gameplay, this tournament is bound to leave you on the edge of your seat. Get ready to show off your tactical skills and dominate the battlefield!

Quick Think: Impromptu Challenge

How fast can you think on your feet? QuickThink is an impromptu problem-solving competition designed to test your ability to come up with creative solutions to unexpected challenges. Participants will face a series of quick challenges that demand fast decision-making, creativity, and agility.

Quizzie Quest: Tech Quiz

Do you have a wealth of tech knowledge? QuizzieQuest is a fast-paced quiz where participants compete to answer questions about all things tech, from current trends to historical milestones in computing. This quiz will test your breadth of knowledge in the tech world. Think you have what it takes to be the ultimate quiz master?
