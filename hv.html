The Ultimate Hackathon

HackVenture is the perfect platform for teams of developers, designers, and innovators to come together and build cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems. Over a span of 2 hours, you will work collaboratively to create a project from scratch, showcasing your technical creativity and teamwork skills. Hackventure welcomes all!

Event Details

  • Date: 4th January 2025
  • Time: 09:00 AM - 11:00 AM
  • Venue: 1st Floor Computer Lab
  • Registration Fee: ₹50 per person
  • Team Size: 2 participants per team

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Gold Medal, ₹1,000 Cash Prize.
  • 2nd Place: Silver Medal.
  • 3rd Place: Bronze Medal.
