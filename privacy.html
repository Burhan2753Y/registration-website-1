Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Technovation collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide during event registration and participation.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:
Name
Email address
Contact number
Institution/Organization details
Other information required for event registration or participation.

3. Use of Personal Information

The personal information collected will be used solely for the following purposes:
Managing event registrations and participation.
Communicating important updates, schedules, and changes related to Technovation.
Facilitating smooth event organization and logistics.
Providing post-event feedback surveys and certificates, if applicable.

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your personal information remains secure. Appropriate technical and administrative measures are in place to safeguard data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

5. Information Sharing

No third-party sharing: Your personal data will not be shared, sold, or distributed to third parties. Information will only be accessible to the event organizers and authorized personnel for event-related activities.

6. Data Retention

Your personal information will be retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Once the event concludes, data may be securely deleted, unless required for legal or record-keeping purposes.

7. Amendments to the Privacy Policy

Technovation organizers reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Participants will be notified promptly of any changes through email or official event announcements.

8. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the use of your personal information, please contact us:

Email: technovation@burhanicollege.edu Event Website: Technovation Registration
