Rules & Guidelines

  • Wearing the college ID is compulsory for all participants during the event.
  • Team Structure
    • Teams must consist of four players.
    • Substitutes are not allowed once the tournament starts.
  • Game Rules
    • All matches will be played on the specified map chosen by the organizers.
    • Each team must use their own devices to play, ensuring they meet the minimum system requirements.
    • Teams will accumulate points based on kills, placements, and other criteria set by the organizers.
  • Match Format
    • Matches will be played in a battle royale format with a specified duration. The team with the most points wins.
  • Collaboration/Communication
    • Collaboration or communication between teams is prohibited during gameplay.
    • Teams must use in-game voice chat only for coordination.
  • Cheating and Fair Play
    • Cheating, including the use of third-party software, or Jammer devices will result in disqualification.
    • Harassment or inappropriate behavior will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Equipment and Internet Usage
    • Players must use their own gaming equipment with stable internet connections.
  • Judging and Results
    • The final decision of the judges is final and binding.
