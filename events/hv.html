Welcome to Hackventure!

HackVenture is the perfect platform for teams of developers, designers, and innovators to come together and build cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems. Over a span of 2 hours, you will work collaboratively to create a project from scratch, showcasing your technical creativity and teamwork skills. Hackventure welcomes all!

Event Details

  • Date: 4th January 2025
  • Time: 09:00 AM - 11:00 AM
  • Venue: 1st Floor Computer Lab
  • Registration Fee: ₹100 per Duo
  • Team Size: 2 participants per team

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Gold Medal + Cash Prize.
  • 2nd Place: Silver Medal.
  • 3rd Place: Bronze Medal.

What is Hackventure?

  • Hackventure is a problem solving event where you will be tasked with a web development assignment. Where in 2 hours you will explore learn,solve and challenge yourself against time and skills. It requires collaborative learning and development.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Wearing the college ID is compulsory for all participants during the event.
  • Team Structure
    • Teams must consist of two participants.
  • Project Guidelines
    • Projects must align with the specified theme or problem statement.
    • The Hackathon has a strict time limit for project development.
    • All code must be original and created solely by the team.
    • Pre-existing code is not allowed, except for open-source libraries or frameworks.
    • Participants must not use code or assets created by others without proper permissions or licenses.
  • Device and Internet Usage
    • Internet usage is restricted to importing libraries, not for troubleshooting.
    • AI tools, including ChatGPT, are prohibited.
    • Referring to documentation is not allowed.
    • No mobile or smart devices are allowed during the Hackathon.
    • Pen drives are not permitted.
  • Collaboration/Communication
    • Collaboration between teams is prohibited.
    • Communication between teams is prohibited.
  • Presentation and Judging
    • Each team must present their project to the judging panel, showcasing its functionality, features, and innovative aspects.
    • Judging criteria: innovation, technical implementation, user experience, scalability, and relevance to the theme.
    • The judging panel’s decision is final.
  • Submission and Code Validation
    • Teams must submit their code and documentation by the deadline.
    • Organizers may validate code for rule compliance.
  • Ethical Standards
    • Participants must uphold ethical standards; any cheating or unfair practices will lead to disqualification.
    • Harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior will result in disqualification.
