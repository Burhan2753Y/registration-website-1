Welcome to QuickThink!

How fast can you think on your feet? QuickThink is an impromptu problem-solving competition designed to test your ability to come up with creative solutions to unexpected challenges. Participants will face a series of quick challenges that demand fast decision-making, creativity, and agility.

Event Details

  • Date: 4th January 2025
  • Time: 12 PM - 1 PM
  • Venue: Auditorium
  • Registration Fee: FREE
  • Team Size: 1 Participant

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Gold Medal.
  • 2nd Place: Silver Medal.
  • 3rd Place: Bronze Medal.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Wearing the college ID is compulsory for all participants during the event.
  • Team Structure
    • Each participant competes individually.
    • No team-based participation is allowed for this event.
  • Game Rules
    • Topics will be drawn randomly from a bowl at the beginning of each round.
    • Participants will have 30 seconds to prepare and 1 minute to present their response or solution.
    • Responses can be delivered in any format (speech, demonstration, or short performance) depending on the topic.
  • Collaboration
    • Collaboration between participants is prohibited during the event.
    • Each participant must respond independently to their assigned topic.
  • Ethical Standards
    • Participants must maintain respectful behavior at all times during the event.
    • Any form of harassment or inappropriate behavior will lead to disqualification.
  • Judging and Results
    • Judging will be based on creativity, clarity, and the ability to think and present on the spot.
    • The final decision of the judges is final and binding.
