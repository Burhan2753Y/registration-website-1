Welcome to Code In The Dark!

This intense coding battle will put your coding skills to the ultimate test. With no access to previews, you will code a web page based solely on a given design. It's all about precision and speed, but there's a catch: you won’t see your progress until time’s up! This challenge is not for the faint-hearted but perfect for those who thrive under pressure.

Event Details

  • Date: 4th January 2025
  • Time: 12 PM - 1 PM
  • Venue: 1st Floor Computer Lab
  • Registration Fee: FREE
  • Team Size: 1 Participant

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Gold Medal.
  • 2nd Place: Silver Medal.
  • 3rd Place: Bronze Medal.

What Will Happen?

  • Objective: Participants will solve a coding problem with their computer monitor turned off.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Wearing the college ID is compulsory for all participants during the event.
  • Team Structure
    • Each participant competes individually.
  • Game Rules
    • Participants must write code with their monitors off, relying only on memory and typing.
    • Only the provided editor will be available; no access to external tools or documentation.
    • The task will be shared at the beginning, and participants must complete it within the specified time limit.
  • Code and Submission
    • Participants must submit their code before the deadline.
    • Code must be original and written solely by the participant during the event.
  • Collaboration
    • Collaboration between participants is strictly prohibited.
    • Participants must not seek or provide assistance during the event.
  • Ethical Standards
    • Cheating or any unfair advantage, including the use of external help, will result in disqualification.
    • Participants must treat all competitors with respect, and harassment or inappropriate behavior will lead to disqualification.
  • Judging and Results
    • The judging will be based on the correctness, functionality, and efficiency of the submitted code.
    • The final decision of the judges is final and binding.
