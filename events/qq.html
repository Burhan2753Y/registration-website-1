Welcome to QuizzieQuest!

Do you have a wealth of tech knowledge? QuizzieQuest is a fast-paced quiz where participants compete to answer questions about all things tech, from current trends to historical milestones in computing. This quiz will test your breadth of knowledge in the tech world. Think you have what it takes to be the ultimate quiz master?

Event Details

  • Date: 4th January 2025
  • Time: 11 AM - 12 PM
  • Venue: 1st Floor Computer Lab
  • Registration Fee: ₹30 per person
  • Team Size: 1 participant

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Gold Medal + Cash Prize.
  • 2nd Place: Silver Medal.
  • 3rd Place: Bronze Medal.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Wearing the college ID is compulsory for all participants during the event.
  • Team Structure
    • Each participant competes individually.
  • Game Rules
    • Questions will be displayed on the big screen, and participants must answer using their phones or other mobile devices.
    • Participants must ensure they have a stable internet connection to participate.
    • Each question will have a time limit, and participants must select their answers before the time runs out.
  • Collaboration
    • Collaboration between participants is allowed only within the same team.
    • Participants must not communicate with other teams or individuals during the quiz.
  • Cheating and Fair Play
    • Using external help, such as searching answers online or asking others, is strictly prohibited.
    • Any form of cheating will result in disqualification.
  • Ethical Standards
    • Respectful behavior towards other participants and organizers is expected at all times.
    • Harassment or inappropriate behavior will result in disqualification.
  • Scoring and Results
    • Scores will be awarded based on the correctness and speed of the answers.
    • The team or participant with the highest score at the end of the quiz will be declared the winner.
    • The final decision of the judges is final and binding.
